Our Story

More Than
Just a Café

Nestled in the heart of East Barnet Village, Café No.41 is where the neighbourhood gathers. Whether you're grabbing your morning espresso before the commute, settling in for brunch with friends, or finding your quiet corner with a laptop — this is your space.

We open early so your day starts right. We close when the village settles. And in between, we pour our heart into every cup, plate, and moment.
