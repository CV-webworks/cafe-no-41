Our Story

More Than Just Coffee

Café No.41 is a locally loved gathering spot in the heart of East Barnet Village. We believe in simplicity done right—premium ingredients, skilled preparation, and genuine hospitality that makes every visit feel special.

From early morning commuters grabbing their first coffee to friends meeting for a leisurely breakfast, we're here to fuel your day with quality, warmth, and care. Every dish is made fresh, every coffee is crafted with precision.

Open Monday to Saturday from 6:15am to 5pm, we're proud to be part of your daily routine and the heartbeat of our community.
