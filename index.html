Our Story

More Than Just Coffee

Café No.41 is a locally loved gathering spot in the heart of East Barnet Village. We believe in simplicity done right—premium ingredients, skilled preparation, and genuine hospitality.

From early morning commuters to friends meeting for brunch, we're here to fuel your day with quality, warmth, and care.

Open Monday to Saturday from 6:15am to 5pm, we're proud to be part of your daily routine.
